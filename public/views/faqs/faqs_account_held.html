Where is my money held ?
Your assets and money are held with Apex Clearing Corporation, which provides the brokerage services for IQ Vestment accounts.
Apex is a proven leader in the clearing space, led by a leadership team that brings over 150 years of combined experience in the space. That experience is combined with a relentless focus on our customers, an ability to anticipate industry trends, and a sophisticated suite of forward-thinking technology-driven solutions. This powerful combination enables us to work together in a highly consultative way, perfectly aligned to help you solve your most challenging business problems. Ultimately, our goal is to empower your innovation.
Apex is consistently selected as the clearing firm for tech savvy, Fintech firms in the space. As a first-mover in the Fintech space, Apex has become the Custodian-of-Choice for tech-savvy firms like Betterment, Stash and other financial disrupters.
You can always view your account number by log in, wording at the top of the IQ Vestment webpage or also on the direct login page.
