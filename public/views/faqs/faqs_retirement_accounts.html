Does IQ Vestment accept retirement accounts?
Yes. IQ Vestment will accept your retirement accounts from another institution, brokerage or wealth management firm. The process varies depending on the type of retirement account you currently have. Employer-sponsored retirement plans
Traditional, Roth, and SEP IRAs
If you have an existing Traditional, Roth, or SEP IRA account and have decided to move out of that account, you can transfer that account, tax-free to IQ Vestment. For more details on IRA rollovers visit the IRS website
Once you're ready to move an account over, learn how to to get started with transferring your existing IRA to IQ vestment.
Employee-sponsored plan
If you have a 401(k), 403(b), 457, TSP, or other employer-sponsored retirement plan with a former employer and have decided to move out of that plan, you can roll it over to a IQ Vestment IRA account.
Note: Rollovers are typically from former employers. You cannot roll over your account if it's in a plan that is sponsored by a current employer, unless your plan administrator allows "in-service" rollovers. Click here for more information about in-service rollovers.
