Rollover: Can I roll over a 401(k), 403(b) or TSP into an IRA?
Yes you can ! You may roll over a 401(k), 403(b), 457, TSP, or other employer-sponsored retirement plan into an IRA at IQ Vestment.
If you are a already a client, simply click "Transfer / rollover" on your dashboard after you login:
Note: Rollovers are typically from former employers. You cannot roll over your account if it's in a plan that is sponsored by a current employer.
To get started with your rollover, please follow these instructions:
  • If you are already a IQ Vestment client, simply login in and click the "Transfer Account In / rollover" button on your dashboard to begin the process.
Note: if you have a traditional 401(k), you need a traditional IRA here at IQ Vestment. If you have a Roth 401(k), you need a Roth IRA at IQ Vestment.
  • If you aren't already a client, simply click "Get Started" on www.IQVestment.com
and then select the “Roll over” option on the account selection page of the account opening process.
  • Once your IRA account is open, you should contact your retirement plan provider and instruct them to send a check for the entire value of your plan.
Note: Some administrators may allow you to send funds via wire transfer. See wire instructions below.
