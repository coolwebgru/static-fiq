IQ Methodology
Smarter Analytics. Smarter Investing.
IQ Vestment's Adaptive Dynamic Portfolios (ADPs) are automated portfolios that dynamically refactor the portfolio decision drivers (inputs) in the model's forensic, four dimensional algorithm, based on changing market conditions.
This deep analysis of portfolio holdings is based on four major investment themes; fundamentals, technicals, sentiment, and momentum. Drawing form a pool close to 100 factors, ADPs typically use anywhere from 30 - 50 factors in analyzing a stock for inclusion into a portfolio. This unbiased, unemotional, repeatable process allows for flexibility, consistency and evidence-based analysis.
In summary, ADPs are portfolios that identify changes in market structure then automatically adapt the portfolios to these changes.
A portfolio methodology founded on deep, forensic analytics
The methodology behind IQ Vestment's efficacy were validated by an independent expert research company. The analysis found that FusionIQ Master Scores above 70 and below 45 show valuable and consistent results over many different investment capitalizations and styles. Over the period 2013 through 2015 using a liquid universe with market capitalization above $200 million, stocks with a FusionIQ Master Score of 70 or more outperformed the universe 62% of the time, while stocks with a FusionIQ Master Score of less than 45 underperformed the universe 65% of the time. The average monthly model portfolio return for stocks, rebalanced monthly, with a FusionIQ Master Score of 70 or more was 35 bps above the universe while the average monthly return for stocks with a FusionIQ Master Score less than 45 was 72 bps below the universe.
